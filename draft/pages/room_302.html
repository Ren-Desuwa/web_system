BSCS Room

BSCS refers to a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. It's a four-year undergraduate degree program that focuses on the study of computing concepts, theories, algorithmic foundations, and the application of information and computing solutions. BSCS programs typically prepare students for careers in IT, software development, research, and other areas requiring a strong understanding of computer science.
